Description

Indulge in the wholesome goodness of CleanEatz’s Nutty Millet Mix, a premium blend designed to elevate your nutritional intake. Our Nutty Millet Mix is meticulously crafted from a diverse combination of millet grains, enriched with the richness of almonds, walnuts, pistachios, and a hint of aromatic cardamom. This exceptional mix offers a powerhouse of fiber, protein, and vital nutrients, ensuring a perfect balance of taste and health in every bite.

Key Ingredients:

  • Millet Grains: A nutritious base rich in fiber and essential minerals.
  • Almonds: A source of healthy fats, vitamin E, and antioxidants.
  • Walnuts: Packed with omega-3 fatty acids and brain-boosting benefits.
  • Pistachios: High in protein and fiber, promoting heart health.
  • Cardamom: Adds a touch of warmth and aids in digestion.

Benefits:

  • Nutrient-Dense: Combines the best of millet and nuts to deliver a wholesome snack packed with essential vitamins and minerals.
  • High in Fiber: Supports digestive health and helps maintain a healthy weight.
  • Protein-Rich: Ideal for muscle repair and growth.
  • Heart-Healthy: Ingredients like walnuts and almonds contribute to overall cardiovascular health.
  • Deliciously Versatile: Perfect as a snack, breakfast cereal, or a nutritious addition to smoothies and yogurt.

Directions to Cook:
1. Boil: Boil 1 glass of water (200 ml) in a vessel.
2. Mix: Take some cold water in a bowl and mix 2 tbsp (15g) of Magic Millet Mix into it without any lumps.
3. Combine: Pour the mixture into boiling water and stir continuously until it thickens (5-10 min).
4. Sweeten: Add 1 tbsp of jaggery and serve hot.

Note: Transfer this mix to an air-tight jar after opening for long-lasting freshness and aroma.

Elevate your snacking experience with the Nutty Millet Mix from CleanEatz. Savor the perfect blend of taste and health in every spoonful.
