]]> Discover the magic of optimal health with CleanEatz’s Magic Millet Mix. This unique blend combines the finest millet grains with the natural sweetness of dry dates and the aromatic essence of cardamom. Our Magic Millet Mix is an exceptional source of fiber, protein, and essential nutrients, offering a delicious and nutritious boost to your daily diet.

Key Ingredients:

  • Millet Grains: Provides a robust foundation of fiber and minerals.
  • Dry Dates: Naturally sweet, rich in iron and potassium.
  • Cardamom: Enhances flavor while aiding digestion and promoting overall wellness.

Benefits:

  • Nutrient-Rich: A perfect balance of vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants.
  • Fiber-Loaded: Promotes digestive health and helps regulate blood sugar levels.
  • Natural Sweetness: The dry dates provide a healthy alternative to refined sugars.
  • Protein-Packed: Supports muscle growth and maintenance.
  • Versatile Use: Ideal for breakfast, snacks, or as an ingredient in various recipes.
  • Blood Sugar Management: With a low glycemic index, it helps stabilize blood sugar levels, suitable for diabetes management.
  • Antioxidant Boost: Rich in antioxidants from millets, sorghum, dry dates, and cardamom, aiding in reducing inflammation and fighting diseases.

 

Directions to Cook:
1. Boil: Boil 1 glass of water (200 ml) in a vessel.
2. Mix: Take some cold water in a bowl and mix 2 tbsp (15g) of Magic Millet Mix into it without any lumps.
3. Combine: Pour the mixture into boiling water and stir continuously until it thickens (5-10 min).
4. Sweeten: Add 1 tbsp of jaggery and serve hot.

Note: Transfer this mix to an air-tight jar after opening for long-lasting freshness and aroma.

Experience the blend of tradition and nutrition with the Magic Millet Mix from CleanEatz. Enjoy the delightful fusion of health and flavor in every serving.
